Who is Velour the Label?

Velour the Label is a black-owned women's clothing brand established in 2020 by a young entrepreneur at the age of 19. Specializing in athleisure and loungewear, we focus on the intersection of quality, comfort, and style. Our current release is the Seamless collection, featuring leggings, shorts, and tops meticulously crafted without seams for a sleek and comfortable fit. We're all about colors, offering our Seamless Collection in Burnt Umber, Pastel Teal, and Cream. Want a complete look? We've got you covered with sets that include matching tops with the option of shorts or leggings. Let's make comfort and style our mantra, shall we?
